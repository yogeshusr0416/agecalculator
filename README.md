**OVERVIEW**

The Age Calculator is a simple application that calculates a person’s age based on their date of birth. It can display the age in years, months, and days, and is useful for quick checks or as a utility in larger applications.

**Features**
- Input date of birth and get exact age.
- Age displayed in years, months, and days.
- Handles leap years and different month lengths.
- Simple and user-friendly interface.
- Lightweight and easy to integrate into other projects.
